ℹ️ KTMB Ticket Refund & Cancellation Policy
> 24 Hours Before Departure

Eligible for a 70% refund of the ticket base price. 30% cancellation administrative charge applies.

2 - 24 Hours Before Departure

Eligible for a 50% refund of the ticket base price. 50% cancellation fee applies.

< 2 Hours or After Train Departure

Tickets are strictly non-refundable and non-cancellable.

Train Driver (Train Captain)

Kuala Lumpur HQ Full-Time RM 3,500 - RM 5,000 / mo

Responsible for the safe, punctual operation of passenger electric trains (ETS) and Komuter lines adhering strictly to signals, safety guidelines, and schedule guidelines.

Requirements:
  • Diploma or SPM with credit in Physics and Mathematics
  • Good color vision and physical fitness (Class-1 health standards)
  • Willingness to work flexible shift hours

Station Operations Executive

Butterworth Station Full-Time RM 2,800 - RM 4,200 / mo

Oversee day-to-day operations at the station platform, passenger gate controls, customer information desks, ticketing counters, and coordinate response during delays.

Requirements:
  • Bachelor's Degree in Logistics, Business Admin, or related
  • Excellent communication skills in Malay and English
  • Strong problem-solving ability under high passenger volumes

Customer Support Representative

KL Sentral Office Shift Rotation RM 2,500 - RM 3,800 / mo

  • Basic computer literacy and ticket entry systems experience
